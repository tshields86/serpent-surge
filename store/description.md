# Serpent Surge

Navigate a neon serpent through increasingly dangerous arenas in this roguelike reimagining of Snake. Eat food to grow, clear waves, and choose power-ups to shape each run.

## Key Features

- Wave-based progression with escalating difficulty
- 15 unique power-ups with stacking and synergy combos
- Boss arenas every 5th level
- 5 hazard types: wall blocks, spike traps, poison trails, warp holes, magnets
- 5 food types with unique effects
- Meta progression: earn scales to unlock abilities and skins
- 17 achievements to discover
- Daily Challenge mode with seeded runs
- Endless mode for high-score chasers
- Retro CRT aesthetic with synthwave soundtrack
- 8 cosmetic snake skins

Built with love for the arcade era.
