Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

Serpent Surge ("the Game") is developed by Travis Shields. This policy describes what data the Game collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data Stored on Your Device

The Game saves the following data locally on your device using IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device unless you use online features (see below).

  • High score, total runs, and in-game currency (Scales)
  • Unlocked skins and achievements
  • Settings (audio volume, visual preferences)
  • Player name — a short display name or initials you choose (3–10 characters)
  • Daily Challenge best score

Online Features (Optional)

If online features are available, the Game may use Google Firebase to provide leaderboards and anonymous gameplay analytics. When you submit a leaderboard score, the following is sent to Firebase:

  • Your chosen display name or initials (not a real name)
  • Score, arenas cleared, and food eaten for that run
  • Whether the run was a Daily Challenge
  • A timestamp

Firebase assigns an anonymous authentication token to your device. No email, real name, or other personal identifier is collected or required.

Analytics

The Game may collect anonymous gameplay statistics (scores, arenas reached, power-ups used) to help improve game balance. These events contain no personally identifiable information and cannot be linked back to you.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Firestore database, anonymous authentication. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — The "Press Start 2P" font is loaded from Google Fonts. Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The Game does not require any personal information to play.

Data Retention & Deletion

Local data can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser storage or uninstalling the app. Leaderboard entries stored in Firebase are retained indefinitely. To request deletion of your leaderboard data, contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date at the top of the page will be revised accordingly.
