## Full Description (both stores)

Serpent Surge takes the classic Snake formula and supercharges it with roguelike progression, strategic power-ups, and a retro CRT aesthetic that glows.

**How it plays:**
- Guide your snake through escalating arenas filled with food, hazards, and surprises
- Collect Scales to unlock new skins and upgrades between runs
- Choose powerful power-ups after each wave — stack them for devastating synergies
- Compete on global leaderboards or challenge yourself with the Daily Challenge (same seed for everyone)

**What makes it different:**
- Roguelike structure: every run is unique, every death is a lesson
- Strategic depth: choose your power-up build wisely — some combos are game-changing
- Retro-futuristic visuals: CRT scanlines, neon glow effects, and pixel-perfect rendering
- One-thumb controls: swipe to play — designed for mobile from the ground up
- Offline-first: play anywhere, anytime — no internet required

**Features:**
- Multiple arenas with increasing difficulty
- Dozens of power-ups with unique synergy combinations
- Daily Challenge mode with shared leaderboards
- Unlockable snake skins and cosmetics
- Synthesized retro sound effects
- Works offline — your progress saves locally

No ads. No pay-to-win. Just pure arcade action.
